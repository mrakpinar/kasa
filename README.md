    <h1>Kasa</h1>
  </div>

  <p>Kasa is a mobile app designed to help you manage your budget by tracking your income and expenses. Easily record your financial transactions and gain insights into your spending habits. The app provides a user-friendly interface and powerful features to help you stay on top of your finances.</p>

  <h2>Features</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Track your incomes and expenses easily with intuitive input forms.</li>
    <li>View your financial data in visually appealing charts and graphs.</li>
    <li>Organize your transactions by categories for better insights.</li>
    <li>Secure your data with local storage.</li>
    <li>Switch between light and dark themes to suit your preference.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Getting Started</h2>
  <p>This project is a starting point for a Flutter application. Follow the steps below to set up and run the app on your local machine.</p>

  <h3>Prerequisites</h3>
  <ul>
    <li><a href="https://flutter.dev/docs/get-started/install">Flutter</a></li>
    <li><a href="https://dart.dev/get-dart">Dart</a></li>
  </ul>

  <h3>Installation</h3>
  <pre>
    <code>
git clone https://github.com/username/kasa.git
    </code>
  </pre>
  <pre>
    <code>
cd kasa
    </code>
  </pre>
  <pre>
    <code>
flutter pub get
    </code>
  </pre>
  <pre>
    <code>
flutter run
    </code>
  </pre>
